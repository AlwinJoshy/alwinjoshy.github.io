AI Film Making

in the year of our lord 2024, what is all that is talked about if not AI generated content and the future of art and creative industries once dominated by human creativity and artistry. This is all I been seeing for months and months without end, but I never fet it was good enough to worth my persuit as the quality appears alwaty lacking in precession or even anything remotely toleerable to say the least, untill very recently. It seem like in the past 6 months there seem to be more involvement and attention by bigger companies as well as startups attemptenibg to make realtime video generation AI, realtinme Game AI etc. so to me its a turn in the right direction. A step to the next phace of AI conquest, where it will soon dominate the creative industry as well.


So I decided to try it out for the first time and here are the steps.

  • you generate an image generateion prompt using LLM(large language model) like ChatGPT, Nemotron or Claud
  • then you can us the prompt on Image generation diffussion model provided by any service out there like OpenArt, Deepia, Adobe AI, Midjourney or Stable Diffussion
  • Finally, you take the image and pass it through an video generation AI like Minmax, Runway or Krea as the starting frame and pass in prompts to guide the video generation

to start off I had decided to go with ChatGpt to generate prompts for generation images and whatever text generation is required and to maintain consistency through the image generation regarding the style and the theme of each generated images.

first of all I went to google images to find an image that displays the style and vibe I was looking for in my "Movie". anything that closely resemble to your vision. I was looking up film Noir movie scenes.

then I copied one of the images and pased it onto ChatGPT and asked it to give me a promt so that I can pass it into an image generator and generate images with similar style

Once I extracted the style of the image and the overall aesthestics of the image using chatgpt. I was looking around to find a free AI image generateor as I assumed it takes a lot of generations to get my images right to how I wanted. Lucky i found deepai.org image generator which allow me to generate an indefinite amount of images for absolutely free. which I used to make several images for the same prompt to get the best one and to roughely maintain consistency at least in in terms of noticable visial elements

Once I was satisfied with the result. I went to the new Chinese made free to use AI video generator hailuoai by Minmax. which produces impressive results all for absolutely free of charge.

I mostly let the AI deside to generate on its own and not guide it except for a few cases. but I must say Minmax offers much more control over how its looks compared to the image generation stage.

and Finally after making several images to fill out the content of my somewhat spontaneous story-board. I downloaded them all and stiched them togather using CapCut

Here is the stiched up videos uploaded to Youtube with added Noir Jazz music so it flows well like a trailer. I feel like it went well for a first project as a test run. there are lot more to improve expecially in aspects that determines consistency.
